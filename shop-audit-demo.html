Core Issues
  • GMV down 15% while posting held steady 28 posts in June vs 28 in May — each video is converting less. GMV per post fell from $1,463 to $1,242.
  • Revenue mix swung hard to TAP TAP went from 23% of GMV to 86% in one month. Non-TAP shop sales collapsed — the products that drove May aren't being reposted.
  • December's winner was never replaced $74.7K in Dec fell to a $17.2K floor by April. No single product has been scaled to fill that gap since.
Fixes
  • Repost your winners 1 winning product = multiple posts. Don't retire a product while it's still converting.
  • Stay in one lane Your audience buys Fashion. Keep 80% of posts in-category; test outside it with at most 1 in 5.
  • Tighten the CTA Add urgency in the first 3 seconds and pin the product link comment on every video.
